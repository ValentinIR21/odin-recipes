Caramel Frosting

Total cooking time:

15 mins

Servings:

12

Frosting Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter

  • ½ cup packed brown sugar

  • 3 tablespoons milk, or more as needed

  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar

  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  • Step 1

    Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar and 3 tablespoons milk. Boil vigorously for 1 minute.

  • Step 2

    Remove from heat and stir in 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar. Cool slightly, then beat in vanilla and remaining 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar. Add more milk if mixture is too thick.
